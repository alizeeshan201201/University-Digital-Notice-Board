Important Instructions

  • Students must arrive at the examination venue 30 minutes before the scheduled time.
  • Carry your university ID card and examination hall ticket without fail.
  • Mobile phones, smart watches, and electronic devices are strictly prohibited.
  • Students found indulging in malpractice will be subject to disciplinary action as per HEC rules.
  • No student will be allowed to enter the examination hall after 15 minutes of commencement.
  • Students with special requirements should contact the examination office at least 48 hours prior.
  • Examination results will be announced within 3 weeks after the completion of all exams.
  • Revaluation applications must be submitted within 7 days of result declaration.
