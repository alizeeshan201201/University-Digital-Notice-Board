Academic Notices

Stay updated with the latest announcements and important information from University of Central Punjab.

15 January, 2024

Spring 2024 Academic Notices

Examination Schedule Released

The examination schedule for the Spring 2024 semester has been released. Students are advised to check their respective department timetables and prepare accordingly. Detailed schedule is available on the university portal.

12 January, 2024

Library Hours Extended During Exam Period

The university library will remain open from 8:00 AM to 11:00 PM during the examination period to provide students with additional study time. Special study areas have been designated for group discussions. HEC digital resources are available 24/7.

10 January, 2024

Registration for Summer Courses Now Open

Registration for summer courses and workshops is now open. Students can register online through the student portal. Early bird discount available until 25 January, 2024. Courses approved by HEC Pakistan.

8 January, 2024

Guest Lecture on Artificial Intelligence

Department of Computer Science is organizing a guest lecture on "Recent Advances in Artificial Intelligence" by Dr. Ahmed Hassan from NUST Islamabad. The lecture is scheduled for 20 January, 2024, at 2:00 PM in the Main Auditorium.

5 January, 2024

Scholarship Applications Deadline Extended

The deadline for HEC merit-based scholarship applications has been extended to 30 January, 2024. Interested students should submit their applications with all required documents to the Financial Aid Office. Punjab Educational Endowment Fund scholarships also available.

3 January, 2024

Campus Recruitment Drive - February 2024

Major Pakistani companies including Engro, PTCL, and Systems Limited will be visiting campus for recruitment in February 2024. Students should update their resumes and register with the Placement Cell. Pre-placement training sessions will begin from 15 January, 2024.

28 December, 2023

New Research Center Inauguration

The university is inaugurating a new Research Center for Sustainable Development on 25 January, 2024. The center will focus on environmental research and sustainable technologies relevant to Pakistan's development goals.

25 December, 2023

Winter Break Schedule Announced

The university will observe winter break from 25 December, 2023, to 7 January, 2024. Administrative offices will remain open with limited staff. Emergency services will be available 24/7. Online classes will continue as per schedule.

20 December, 2023

Student Exchange Program Applications

Applications for the Spring 2024 Student Exchange Program are now being accepted. Selected students will have the opportunity to study at partner universities in Turkey, Malaysia, and Saudi Arabia.

18 December, 2023

Annual Sports Meet Registration

Registration for the Annual Sports Meet is now open. Events include cricket, hockey, football, athletics, basketball, volleyball, chess, and table tennis. Students can register through the Sports Department office.

15 December, 2023

New Course Introduction - Data Science

The Department of Mathematics and Computer Science is introducing a new interdisciplinary course in Data Science starting Spring 2024. The course will cover machine learning, statistical analysis, and big data technologies with focus on Pakistani industry applications.

12 December, 2023

Campus Wi-Fi Upgrade Completed

The campus-wide Wi-Fi network has been upgraded to provide faster internet speeds and better coverage. Students can connect using their university credentials for unlimited access. PTCL fiber optic connection ensures high-speed internet.
