Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Programs

  • Minimum 60% aggregate in FSc/A-Levels or equivalent examination
  • Valid scores in UCP entrance examination or provincial entry tests
  • English proficiency: IELTS 6.5+ or TOEFL 80+ (for international students)
  • Age limit: Not exceeding 25 years as of admission date

Graduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field with minimum 65% aggregate
  • Valid scores in UCP entrance examination or GAT/GRE
  • Minimum 2 years of work experience (for MBA program)
  • English proficiency: IELTS 7.0+ or TOEFL 90+ (for international students)

Doctoral Programs

  • Master's degree in relevant field with minimum 70% aggregate
  • Research proposal and publications (if any)
  • Valid scores in UCP entrance examination or HEC tests
  • Interview with departmental research committee
